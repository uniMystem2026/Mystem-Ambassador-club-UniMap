Who We Are

Bridging STEM &
Real-World Impact

The MYSTEM Ambassador Club is the official STEM student body at Universiti Malaysia Perlis (UniMAP). We unite passionate students from engineering, science, and technology faculties to collaborate, innovate, and inspire the wider community through hands-on programmes and cutting-edge projects.

Our work spans robotics, IoT, smart agriculture, AI applications, and outreach to schools across Perlis and northern Malaysia — turning university knowledge into real-world solutions.

What We Do

Our Core Programs

01 / FLAGSHIP
🤖

Robotics & Automation Lab

Hands-on sessions building autonomous robots, PLC systems, and industrial automation rigs. Students compete in regional and national robotics championships.

Robotics PLC Arduino
02 / OUTREACH
🏫

STEM School Outreach

Ambassadors visit secondary schools across Perlis and Kedah to deliver engaging STEM workshops, science fairs, and career guidance for students aged 13–17.

Schools Workshops Mentoring
03 / TECH
🌐

IoT & Smart Systems

Design and deploy Internet-of-Things solutions for smart agriculture, smart campus monitoring, and energy management using Raspberry Pi, ESP32, and cloud platforms.

IoT ESP32 Cloud
04 / AI
🧠

AI & Data Science Track

Bootcamps in Python, machine learning, computer vision, and data analytics — preparing members for the AI-driven Industry 4.0 economy with real project portfolios.

Python ML Data
05 / COMMUNITY
🌱

Tech for Community

Partnering with local farmers, NGOs, and small businesses to apply STEM solutions to real community problems — from precision agriculture to digital literacy drives.

AgriTech NGO Digital
06 / COMPETITION
🏆

Innovation & Competition Hub

Coaching, prototyping support, and funding guidance to help members enter national competitions like iCAR, PECIPTA, MTE, and international hackathons.

PECIPTA MTE Hackathon

Featured Projects

View All →
🌾
Live 2024

AgrioSense — Smart Paddy Field Monitor

A solar-powered IoT sensor network deployed across 3 paddy fields in Perlis, monitoring soil moisture, pH, temperature, and water levels in real-time via a custom mobile dashboard. Reduced water usage by 28%.

🤖
Award 2023

AutoSort — Industrial Vision Sorting Robot

A computer vision–powered conveyor sorting system built for a local manufacturing SME. Uses YOLOv8 on Raspberry Pi 5 to sort defective parts at 95.3% accuracy. Won Silver at PECIPTA 2023.

♻️
National 2024

EcoTrack — Campus Carbon Footprint Dashboard

A real-time web platform tracking energy consumption across UniMAP buildings, calculating CO₂ emissions and gamifying sustainability challenges for students and staff.

🦾
Research 2025

NeuroArm — EMG-Controlled Prosthetic Prototype

A 3D-printed prosthetic arm prototype controlled by electromyography (EMG) signals from muscle activity, developed in collaboration with UniMAP's Biomedical Engineering lab.
