THE STEM ARCADE

9 Advanced Interactive Simulators & Logic Puzzles

💻

Binary Ninja

Comp Sci Logic

Computers think in 1s and 0s. Test your ability to translate decimal numbers into binary code before the system resets!

🧮

Math Sprint

Mathematics Speed

Sharpen your mental arithmetic. Solve as many equations as you can to build a massive high score!

⚡

Reflex Tester

Biology Physics

Measure human reaction time in milliseconds. Wait for the terminal to turn green, then strike as fast as possible!

🪐

Orbit Slingshot

Astrophysics Vectors

A complex physics simulator. Drag to aim and launch your satellite. Use planetary gravity fields to bend your trajectory and hit the target zone!

🔥

Gas Simulator

Chemistry Thermodynamics

Experiment with Kinetic Theory. Increase heat to watch particles gain kinetic energy, expand volume, and monitor pressure changes.

🦠

Game of Life

Algorithms Logic

Conway's famous cellular automaton. Draw a pattern of living cells on the grid, start the simulation, and watch them evolve and die based on logic rules.

🧬

DNA Sequencer

Genetics Biology

Form the double helix! Match falling nucleobases: Adenine (A) pairs with Thymine (T), Cytosine (C) pairs with Guanine (G).

🔦

Laser Optics

Physics Raycasting

A light reflection engine. Drag and rotate mirrors to guide a continuous laser beam from the emitter to the power crystal.

🌿

Fractal Trees

Mathematics Recursion

Discover the math of nature. Manipulate recursion depth and branch angles to generate complex, infinite geometric patterns.
